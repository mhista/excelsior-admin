Excelsior Royal School is a Pre-School Nursery, Primary school located in Owerri Nigeria. A privately owned educational institution that provides unparallel quality education for children between early childhood and formation years. The world in her 21st century of existence, with uncommon discoveries development and drive to outwit each other amongst people, nations, religious and continents of the world, holds in their policy and police that prove that education is the one key tool, medium and proven method of any level of success of they have achieved. At Excelsior Royal School, we have the undisputable and uncompromising believe that your child holds the key to unlock the door that will lead people, nations regions and continents of the world to the next era of human successful existence. We therefore welcome you, your child/ward to the place of self fulfillment and relevance on the world’s stage Excelsior Royal School

OUR MISSION
“Our mission is to show care give quality education and build skills for a lifelong leaving, while creating an environment for self discovery, harnessing talent, inculcating the fear of God, respect for rule of law and order and safety for every child.”

OUR VISION
“Our vision is to build a democratic community of children who have positive attitude, values of justice, tolerance, co-operation and equity, where every child is stimulated and motivated to discover themselves, harness their potentials to the fullest, encouraged to become confident, responsible, active and functional contributors towards Global citizenship by employing the services of well motivated staff and best technology.”

TRANSPORT/LOGISTICS

The school’s management sees the need for easy movement, accessibility and challenge working class parents go through to get their wards to school; taking this to key cognizance, the school provides a school run service that has a fleet of school buses that are conducive, secured, road worthy and driven by experienced, licensed and professional chauffeurs.

TEACHING AND NON-TEACHING STAFF

Raising children needs the best of hands in schools. Teaching and Non-teaching staff makes up the formation of team that can help children succeed. We therefore have a team of well motivated, trained, certified and equipped staff who love children and are experienced in the business of educating children.
